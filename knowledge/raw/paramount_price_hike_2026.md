# Paramount+ to Hike U.S. Prices in January 2026

Source: https://variety.com/2025/tv/news/paramount-to-hike-prices-in-early-2026-1236574467/
Retrieved: 2026-04-28
Published: November 10, 2025

## Summary

David Ellison's Paramount Skydance is planning to spend more on content — and at the same time, it's going to charge more for streaming. The company announced price increases in the U.S. starting January 15, 2026.

## New U.S. Pricing (Effective January 15, 2026)

### Monthly Plans
- Paramount+ Essential (ad-supported): $8.99/month (up $1)
- Paramount+ Premium (ad-free): $13.99/month (up $1)

### Annual Plans
- Essential: $89.99/year ($7.50/month equivalent) — up from $59.99
- Premium: $139.99/year ($11.67/month equivalent) — up from $119.99

## Investment Justification

"Our ongoing investments in Paramount+ are enhancing the value we deliver to consumers. To support this continued investment, we plan to implement price increases in the U.S. early in the first quarter of 2026."

The company also announced upcoming price increases in Canada and Australia.

## Content Investments

### Major Deals
- Seven-year, $7.7 billion deal with UFC for exclusive streaming rights
- Five-year exclusive deal with Matt Stone and Trey Parker ("South Park") worth $1.5 billion
- "South Park" was the top subscriber-acquisition driver for Paramount+ in Q3

### 2026 Programming Investment
Paramount expects to make incremental programming investments of more than $1.5 billion in 2026, including:
- Direct-to-consumer investments in UFC
- Paramount+ originals
- Third-party catalog licensing
- Ramp-up in film slate to 15 movies annually

## Current Subscriber Base

Paramount+ ended September 2025 with 79.1 million subscribers, up from 77.7 million three months earlier.

## Q3 2025 Financial Performance

- DTC revenue: increased 17% year-over-year to $2.17 billion
- Paramount+ revenue: up 24% to $1.04 billion
- Adjusted operating income: $105 million (12% margin) pre-close, $235 million (18% margin) post-close

## Competitive Positioning

"These changes will fuel continued reinvestment in the user experience and deliver an even stronger slate of programming for our customers in the year ahead and beyond," Ellison said.

Even after the January 2026 price hikes, Paramount+ will remain one of the most competitively priced streaming services in the U.S.

## Popular Content

- Original series: "Landman," "Tulsa King"
- Franchises: "South Park," "Star Trek"
- Films: "Mission: Impossible," "The Naked Gun"
- CBS hits: "Tracker," "Survivor"
- Sports: Sunday afternoon NFL games, UEFA Champions League

## Reporting Change

Starting in Q4 2025, the company will count only paid Paramount+ subscribers (excluding free trials). At the end of Q3, free trial subscribers totaled 1.2 million.
