# Paramount+

**Paramount+** is the subscription video-on-demand streaming service owned and operated by Paramount Global (now Paramount Skydance following the merger with Skydance Media). The service combines content from CBS, Paramount Pictures, MTV, Nickelodeon, Comedy Central, BET, and other ViacomCBS properties.

---

## Company Overview

Paramount+ is the streaming arm of Paramount Skydance, the media company formed following the acquisition by David Ellison's Skydance Media. The service trades on the stock market under the ticker **PSKY**.

In a major industry consolidation move announced in 2026, Paramount Skydance is set to acquire Warner Bros. Discovery, with the deal expected to close in Q3 2026. This acquisition will combine Paramount+ with HBO Max and Discovery+, creating a streaming entity with more than **200 million direct-to-consumer subscribers** globally.

### Recent Corporate Developments

- **BET+ Integration**: Paramount acquired Tyler Perry's stake in BET+ and is folding the streamer into Paramount+
- **UFC Rights Deal**: Signed a 7-year, $7.7 billion deal for UFC rights—one of the first major content acquisitions under the Ellison leadership
- **Cost Optimization**: In response to profit pressures, Paramount announced $500 million in cost cuts

## Streaming Strategy

### Content Optimization Approach

In August 2023, Paramount outlined a significant shift in its streaming content strategy, moving away from volume-focused content acquisition toward a more targeted, data-driven approach.

**Key Strategic Pillars:**

| Strategy Element | Description |
|------------------|-------------|
| **Audience Segmentation** | Using internal data to identify and "super serve" carefully-defined specific audience segments |
| **Franchise Focus** | Leaning into established franchises rather than standalone content |
| **Cross-Platform Leverage** | Leveraging content across multiple Paramount platforms |
| **Analytics-Driven Programming** | Using data to deliver "the right content for the right audience at the right time" |

> *"We can get away from—call it a volume focused game—and be more focused on making sure that we have the right content for the right audience at the right time."*
> — Naveen Chopra, CFO (August 2023)

### Content Writedowns

As part of this strategic shift, Paramount has taken writedowns on content removed from Paramount+, reflecting a more disciplined approach to content investment.

### Sports Strategy

Paramount+ has positioned live sports as a key differentiator, highlighted by the **$7.7 billion UFC rights deal** spanning seven years. The platform streams UFC pay-per-view events and Fight Night programming, providing a significant draw for subscribers.

### Historical Context

Paramount+ demonstrated strong early growth following its 2021 rebrand from CBS All Access. In Q1 2021, ViacomCBS reported a quarterly gain of 6 million streaming subscribers, with Paramount+ leading the growth.

### Global Footprint

Paramount+ is currently available in approximately **45 markets** worldwide. The company also operates **SkyShowtime**, a joint venture with Comcast in Europe, into which both companies invested $1 billion (as reported in 2025).

## Competitive Position

### Market Standing

Paramount+ occupies a mid-tier position in the streaming landscape:

| Service | Global Subscribers | Markets Available |
|---------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Netflix | 325+ million | 190 |
| HBO Max | 131.6 million* | 110+ |
| Paramount+ | 78.9 million | ~45 |

*Warner Bros. Discovery total including linear HBO and Discovery+

### Subscriber Overlap Analysis

Research from Antenna (January 2026) reveals significant overlap between Paramount+ and HBO Max in the U.S. market:

- **7.6 million** U.S. subscribers have both HBO Max and Paramount+
- **27.9%** of U.S. HBO Max subscribers already have Paramount+
- **21.1%** of U.S. Paramount+ subscribers also pay for HBO Max
- Nearly **14%** of the combined subscriber pool subscribes to both services

This overlap has significant implications for the planned merger, as subscriber de-duplication will affect combined revenue projections.

### Pending Merger with Warner Bros. Discovery

The announced combination with Warner Bros. Discovery will create a combined entity with:

- **210.5 million** total direct-to-consumer subscribers
- Combined content libraries from HBO, Warner Bros., Discovery, CBS, Paramount, MTV, Nickelodeon, and more
- Expanded global reach across 110+ markets

## Key Business Metrics

### Financial Priorities

Paramount has emphasized two primary areas for streaming profitability:

1. **Content Spend Optimization** — Reducing content costs while maintaining engagement
2. **Marketing Efficiency** — Optimizing the two largest expense categories in the streaming P&L

### Revenue Considerations

Following the WBD merger, ARPU (Average Revenue Per User) is expected to rise as overlapping subscribers are consolidated. The combined service pricing structure remains to be announced but will likely fall between the cost of one service and the combined cost of both.
