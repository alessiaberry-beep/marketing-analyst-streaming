# Paramount+ Wiki Page

## Overview

Paramount+ is a streaming service operated by Paramount Global (stock ticker: PSKY). The service has approximately **78.9 million subscribers** as of the end of 2025, with **35.8 million paying subscribers in the U.S.** as of January 2026.

---

## Key Executives

### David Ellison
- **Title:** Chief Executive, Paramount Skydance
- **Role:** Following the Skydance merger with Paramount, Ellison leads the combined company and has announced plans to acquire Warner Bros. Discovery, which would create a combined entity with over 200 million direct-to-consumer subscribers

### Bob Bakish
- **Title:** Former CEO, Paramount Global
- **Role:** Led Paramount through its streaming strategy evolution in 2023, including the "optimizing content spend" initiative focused on data-driven audience segments and franchise content

### Naveen Chopra
- **Title:** CFO, Paramount Global
- **Role:** Oversaw financial strategy including streaming P&L optimization, focusing on content and marketing efficiency

### Chris McCarthy
- **Title:** Co-CEO, Paramount
- **Role:** Has expressed confidence in Paramount+'s position "to remain a standalone" service

### Damla Dogan
- **Title:** Unscripted Development Executive
- **Previous:** Netflix
- **Role:** Joined Paramount+ in February 2026 to lead unscripted content acquisition and development

- 7.6 million U.S. subscribers have both HBO Max and Paramount+
- 27.9% of HBO Max subscribers also have Paramount+
- 21.1% of Paramount+ subscribers also have HBO Max
- ~14% of combined subscriber pool subscribes to both services

## Strategic Initiatives

### Content Optimization Strategy (2023)
- Shift from volume-focused to audience-segment-focused programming
- Leveraging internal data analytics to "super serve" defined audience segments
- Increased content leverage across multiple platforms
- Greater emphasis on franchise properties
- Content writedowns for removed programming

### Merger & Acquisition Activity
- **Skydance merger** completed; created Paramount Skydance
- **Warner Bros. Discovery acquisition** announced; estimated close Q3 2026
- Combined WBD deal would yield 200+ million DTC subscribers globally

### Cost Reduction
- $500 million cost-cutting initiative announced following profit declines
